About Us

Who We Are

The Rotaract Club of O.P. Jindal Global University is a student-led service organization. We are part of a global network of young professionals and students (Rotaract Global) committed to service and leadership.


Our Purpose

Our purpose is to provide an opportunity for young men and women to enhance the knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development, to address the physical and social needs of their communities, and to promote better relations between all people worldwide through a framework of friendship and service.
